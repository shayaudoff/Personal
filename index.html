A Little About Me

I was born and raised in Rockland County, NY, and spend much of the year down in Ocean County, NJ, where I study and build character at Yeshiva. Beyond that, I’m passionate about exploring new places, meeting different kinds of people, and diving into adventure — whether it’s traveling, mountain biking the trails in Sedona, AZ, or trying something new.

I’ve always been curious and eager to learn, especially about technology, business, and innovation. I’m fascinated by the stock market and entrepreneurship, and I’m currently working toward a degree in Business Science.

This site is just getting started, but over time, it’ll grow into a space that reflects what I’m about — energy, curiosity, and a drive to keep moving forward.

Created for fun, inspiration, and for curing boredom.
