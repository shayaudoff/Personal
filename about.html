About Me

a bit more about me

Hi 👋🏻, my name is Shaya Udoff

...and let me introduce myself.

As Confucius is often contributed to have said
"Choose a job you love, and you never have to work a day in your life"

So with that in mind, I believe entrepreneurship is more than just a career path for me — it's a hobby.

I’ve built multiple ventures from the ground up, including Glisten Glass Art, where I serve as CEO, turning creativity into a business that combines artistry and strategy.

At UB Constructs, as co-founder and CFO, I manage finances and operations, learning how to balance risk and opportunity in real-time.

I also help develop projects at Insiyt as CTO, applying technology and innovation to bring ideas to life.

In addition to my ventures, I actively trade IPOs on Robinhood, exploring the financial markets and understanding how to grow wealth intelligently.

I see every project—whether a startup, an investment, or a new skill—as a chance to challenge myself, learn, and improve.

Studying Business Science allows me to deepen my knowledge of marketing, strategy, and leadership, giving me the tools to take my ideas further and make them sustainable.

I thrive on new challenges and experiences, whether that’s traveling to new places, mountain biking the trails, or hitting the water for wakeboarding and wakesurfing.

My goal is to combine creativity, discipline, and strategic thinking to build ventures that are both successful and meaningful.

Everything I do is part of a larger mission: to learn, grow, and create opportunities that push me forward while inspiring others around me.
